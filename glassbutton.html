Glass Button - Version 1.1

The Glass Button extension gives WYSIWYG Web Builder users an easy way to add trendy glass buttons to their website. The Glass Button has two states: normal and rollover to which you can assign different colors. This extension also support links (website, internal pages and popup windows).
