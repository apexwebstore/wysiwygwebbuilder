Flash Video Player (DEPRECATED)

This extension provides an easy way to insert Jeroen Wijering’s FLV Media Player to your website.
The JW FLV Media Player (built with Adobe's Flash) is an easy and flexible way to add video and audio to your website. It supports playback of any format the Adobe Flash Player can handle (FLV, but also MP3, H264, SWF, JPG, PNG and GIF).

This extension can be used to play single Flash Video Files as well as a playlist with multiple media files.
The appearance of the player is fully configurable through Web Builder, no coding needed!
Download (Last update: February 2, 2011)
Flash Video Player Extension

Requirements:
WYSIWYG Web Builder 5.06 or higher.
Flash Player 7 or higher.
