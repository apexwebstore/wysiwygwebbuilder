Google Text - Version 2.1

The Google Text extension let's you easily create logo's just like the one on www.google.com!
Note: Google used a commercial font for it's logo which is called 'Catull', so unfortunately it is not installed on your PC by default. You can however use an alternative font like 'Book antiqua', which is almost indentical.

New in version 2.00: Text color property and support for links.
