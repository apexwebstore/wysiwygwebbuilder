RSS Parser - Version 1.2

The RSS Parser extension takes the information from a given RSS feed (from another website) and displays the data on your web page. For example, if you want to display the headlines of CNN on your website, you can specify the CNN news feed, but of course there are many more to choose from!

This extension is based on the RSS Feed Reader PHP script by R.J.Kendal. Special thanks to Kees Versluis!
http://www.rjk-hosting.co.uk/programs/prog.php?id=7

Note: If you want to create your own RSS Feed, please take a look at the RSS Feed extension.
