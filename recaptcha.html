reCAPTCHA - Version 1.0 (DEPRECATED)

This extension helps you add reCAPTCHA to your forms without any custom coding.

reCAPTCHA is a free CAPTCHA service that helps protect your site against spam, malicious registrations and other forms of attacks where computers try to disguise themselves as a human. reCAPTCHA is currently one of the best ways to protect online forms from spammers.

Before you can use this extension, you’ll need to sign up at reCAPTCHA.net:
http://www.google.com/recaptcha and create reCAPTCHA keys for your site. The keys are unique to your domain and sub-domains and will not work for other domains. After you have signed up you will receive a Private Key and a Public Key whihc are required by this extension.
