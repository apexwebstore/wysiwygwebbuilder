How can I make your own translation?
If you want to translate WYSIWYG Web Builder into your own language you can download the 'translation pack' here:
https://www.wysiwygwebbuilder.tk/wwb_language.zip
Instructions are included in the readme.txt file.

Alternatively you can download the English template and translate it in your favorite resource editor/translation tool.
https://www.wysiwygwebbuilder.tk/wb15english.zip
