LC Lightbox

 

The LC Lightbox extension is based on lc_lightbox.lite.js by LCweb. https://github.com/LCweb-ita/LC-Lightbox-LITE

LC Lightbox is a modern lightbox focused on contents which is responsive/adaptive and has many options like thumbnail navigation, slideshow, title, description etc.
